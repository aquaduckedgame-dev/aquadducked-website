Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2024

AquaDucked! ("the Game") is created by NickTheDuck. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Levels You Share: When you upload a level to The Pond (our community level browser), we store the level data, the name you provide, and basic metadata like upload date.

Device Information: We may collect anonymous analytics to help improve the game, such as device type and gameplay statistics. This data is not linked to your identity.

Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect personal information like your name, email address, or location. The Game does not require an account to play.

Third-Party Services

The Game may use third-party services for analytics or hosting (such as Supabase for The Pond). These services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at aquaduckedgame@gmail.com.
